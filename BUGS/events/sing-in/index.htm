News Archives

Jonathan is organizing a G&S Sing-In. It will be Saturday, April 29. We'll sing through several G&S shows, and have a grand old time.

We will ask participants to contribute $10 toward refreshments. ($5 for students; zero for accompanists.) This is for snacks and drinks throughout the day, and for lunch. Group dinner arrangements will be for additional cost.
