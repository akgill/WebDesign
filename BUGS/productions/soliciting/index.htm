News Archives

Having recently finished Ruddigore, the most recent BUGS production, we are searching for a director for our spring production of "H.M.S. Pinafore! Auditions for Ruddigore will be held in conjunction with Brown Opera Productions (BOP) on December 6th and 7th, time and place to be announced. "

All information (and I mean ALL information) about "H.M.S. Pinafore" a.k.a "The Lass that Loved a Sailor" can be found at the Gilbert & Sullivan Archive.

Please contact us if you are interested in directing. We welcome all to apply. The deadline for director's applications is 11:30PM December 3rd.

Auditions will take 5-10 minutes. Come prepared to sing a song of your choice and we'll provide the rest (you'll be asked to perform some vocal and diction exercises). For more information, contact Jessica Marquardt.

While the production staff is decided at the discretion of the director and while the director is responsible for finding a production staff, anyone interested in being part of the production staff for our next show is encouraged to contact us.
