ASTROPHYSICS JOURNAL CLUB

The Astrophysics Journal Club meets during the semester to discuss new results, published articles, and topics of general interest to theoretical and experimental cosmologists and astrophysicists. Caffeinated beverages and snacks are provided (and some non-caffeinated beverages as well!).

All Talks are held on Wednesdays at 4 PM in Barus and Holley Room 723.
